State Tax Information

Each state has it's own tax laws and regulations. We still have to pay federal taxes which apply to every state, but what if I told you that you could relocate to a state where cost of living is more affordable because the state taxes are lower. This is the goal of this application, to give you an idea of where you should move in order to pay the least state taxes, which essentially means a better cost of living. This includes looking at state income tax, sales tax, and property tax.
